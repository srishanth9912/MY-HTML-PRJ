What is Daily Expense Tracker?

Daily Expense Tracker is a free, lightweight personal finance tool that runs entirely in your browser. It helps you record, organise, and understand your daily spending — with zero setup, no account, and no internet required.

Pages

  • Home — Add expenses with date, category, amount and note. View the full table and your running total.
  • Monthwise — Pick any month to see all expenses for that period with category-wise totals.
  • Analytics — View summary stats — total, count, average, highest — and category breakdowns over 1, 3, 6 months or all time.

How to Use

  1. On the Home page, enter the date, category, amount, and an optional note — then click Add Expense.
  2. Your expense appears instantly in the table with an updated total.
  3. Go to Monthwise to filter and review spending for any specific month.
  4. Visit Analytics to see stats and breakdowns for different time periods.
  5. Click Delete on any row to remove an expense permanently.

Data & Privacy

All data is stored using your browser's localStorage — entirely on your own device. Nothing is ever sent to a server. Clearing your browser's site data will permanently delete all expenses.
